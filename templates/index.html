Use responsibly

Helpful for learning, not diagnosis

DermAI is designed for educational retrieval over skincare data and one clinical guideline. It should support exploration, not replace a dermatologist or a personalized treatment plan.

Ask by skin type

Examples: “best cleanser for oily skin” or “organic ingredients for sensitive skin.”

Ask by concern

Examples: “what may help with dryness” or “psoriasis topical treatment guidance.”
